Phase 1 Course Goals


1. Foster the creative habits of mind

This course is designed to ignite and nurture creative thinking. Through various projects in digital composition, we explore uncharted territory, think outside the box, and approach problems with innovative solutions. Creative habits of mind are not just about artistic expression but also about finding novel ways to communicate and engage audiences effectively. For example, my abilities as a coder are not the best, but in my work for “Linear web text design”, I created a multi-page treasure hunt story plot. I thought of making a story with the side of having a user interaction.

2. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

Collaboration is a cornerstone of this course. We will actively participate in a workshop environment where we share our work, provide feedback to each other, and engage in constructive discussions. This collaborative spirit enhances our learning experience, allowing us to benefit from diverse perspectives and refine our projects through collective insights. In every workshop, I love looking at my peers' work and asking them how they did it. We have some brilliant coders and artists in our class, unfortunately, I am not one of them, and it is such an experience listening and learning about their work.

3. Think deeply about craft.

Crafting digital compositions involves more than just technical skills. It's about thoughtful and deliberate choices in design, content, and presentation. We students delve into the intricacies of digital craft, analyzing existing compositions to understand the nuances of effective storytelling and communication in the digital realm. For example, the storytelling in my “Linear web text design” in my opinion is a good example of good storytelling, it has the engagement of characters, plot, conflict, resolution, and a theme/ message. I had more ideas when it comes to this project as in a flying dragon attacking and more conflict competencies but my lack of coding experience really held me back.

4. Engage in sustained research and skills development.

To excel in digital composition, we embark on a journey of continuous research and skill development. we explore our chosen themes deeply, gathering relevant information and insights. Simultaneously, we hone our technical skills, mastering various digital tools and platforms necessary for creating impactful digital media. For example, to learn some of the HTML code I had to watch a few videos on Google searching for how to implement basic things. .

5. Learn to present your work

Effective presentation is the key to making a digital composition resonate with its audience. In this course, we will not only create compelling content but also learn how to present it proficiently. This includes multimedia presentations and user interface design, ensuring that their work is not only impactful but also accessible and engaging for a broader audience in the digital landscape. I am not the best presenter and speaker, and I hope by the end of the course I have learned these basic skills.
